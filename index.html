About Me

I'm a PhD researcher working on enhancing fine-grained robotic manipulation through spatial intelligence and bio-inspired learning architectures. My work combines 3D visual fusion, proprioception, and Transformer-based models to advance robotic perception and action understanding.

Research Highlights

  • BFP2AT: Bio-Inspired Fusion-Perception-to-Action Transformer for robotic manipulation.
  • BRISK: Bidirectional Reverse Mamba for contact-rich manipulation learning.
  • Exploring multi-modal 3D representation learning for fine-grained control.
