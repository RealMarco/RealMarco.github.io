About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in a joint program between SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Macau. My research focuses on robot learning, end-to-end multimodal robot manipulation, vision-language-action (VLA), and fine-grained manipulation. I serve as a reviewer for well-known robotics venues, including IEEE TRO, RAL, ICRA, and IROS. I am also currently an intern researcher at Tencent Robotics X Lab.
